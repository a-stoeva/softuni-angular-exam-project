Sunset at Oia Village

Country: Greece

City: Oia

Cost: Free

Time needed: 2–3 hours

Best Time to Visit: Late afternoon

My Experience: Oia is one of the most famous villages in Santorini, known for its stunning sunset views, narrow streets, white houses, and blue domes. I arrived about an hour before sunset and found a spot along the castle ruins. It was crowded, but absolutely worth it. As the sun dipped below the horizon, everyone went silent — it felt surreal.

Valuable Tips: Arrive at least 1 hour early to get a good spot. Avoid peak season if you dislike crowds. Bring water — it can get very hot. Wear comfortable shoes for walking on uneven streets.
